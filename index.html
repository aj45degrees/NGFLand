About New Guard Financial

New Guard Financial (NGF) is a Black-owned, Georgia-based FinTech company with a mission to help small businesses exceed their goals. NGF's experts bring more than a decade of experience to the table and offer a full-service business compliance kit which includes entity creation, bookkeeping, payroll, tax preparation, and more.

After nearly two decades working with the Small Business Association (SBA), former loan officer Rakaia Jackson and partners launched the finTech venture, New Guard Financial, to support small business owners with finanial resilience and PPP loan forgiveness.

Services

New Guard Financial was established in the wake of the financial crisis and has since expanded its operations and services to offer bookkeeping, accounting and payroll services through a mobile-friendly application to streamline loan forgiveness.
